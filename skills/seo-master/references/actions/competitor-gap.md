# /seo-master /competitor-gap

## Purpose

Identify practical opportunities by comparing the target site with search and business competitors.

## Workflow

- Separate SERP competitors from business competitors.
- Compare ranking pages, content formats, topical depth, page templates, internal links, backlinks, and SERP features.
- Find missing pages, weak pages, outdated content, and authority gaps.
- Convert gaps into actions with priority and expected impact.

## Output

- Competitor comparison table
- Gap list by topic, page type, and funnel stage
- Priority opportunities
- Defensive risks
- Recommended next action

Update `project-memory.md`, then update `README.md` Current Status.
